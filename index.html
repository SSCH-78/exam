About

When I was kid, I wanted to be a renaissance woman. Never mind just picking computer science, medicine or literature- I wanted to do everything. Always hungry for more, I chose to become software engineer. Developing and designing a software or website is the craft of describing exactly what you want a tiny chunk of reality to look and act like. You put down this description in an archaic and symbolic language, and then the Magic happens and you get to see your description play out in its own corner of reality.
